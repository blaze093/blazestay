How do I search for rooms on BlazeStay?

You can search for rooms by using the search bar on our homepage. Simply select your preferred city, budget range, and click on "Search Rooms". You can further filter the results by room type, gender preference, and other amenities on the listings page.

Is there any brokerage fee for booking a room?

No, BlazeStay does not charge any brokerage fee. We believe in transparent pricing, and you only pay the rent and security deposit as mentioned on the listing. There are no hidden charges or fees.

How can I contact the room owner?

Once you find a room you're interested in, you can click on "View Details" to see more information. On the room details page, you'll find a "Contact Owner" button. Click on it to send a message or request a call back from the owner. Your contact information will only be shared with the owner after you initiate contact.

Are the rooms verified by BlazeStay?

Yes, all rooms listed on BlazeStay are verified by our team. We ensure that the information provided by the owner is accurate and the room meets our quality standards. Rooms with a "Verified" badge have been physically inspected by our team.

How do I list my room on BlazeStay?

To list your room, you need to sign up as an owner on BlazeStay. After creating your account, you can click on "Add Room" in your dashboard and fill in the details about your property. Once submitted, our team will review and verify your listing before it goes live on the platform.

Is there any fee for listing my room?

No, listing your room on BlazeStay is completely free. We don't charge any commission or fees for connecting you with potential tenants. You receive the full rent amount directly from the tenant.

How does the verification process work?

After you submit your listing, our team will schedule a visit to your property to verify the details provided. We check the room condition, amenities, and take professional photos if needed. This process usually takes 1-3 business days, after which your listing will be published with a "Verified" badge.

What payment methods are accepted?

BlazeStay supports various payment methods including credit/debit cards, net banking, UPI, and wallet payments. For security deposits and monthly rent, you can pay directly to the owner through bank transfer or cash as mutually agreed.

Is there a security deposit for rooms?

Yes, most rooms require a security deposit, which is typically equivalent to 1-2 months' rent. The exact amount is mentioned on each listing. This deposit is refundable at the end of your stay, subject to any deductions for damages or unpaid rent as per the agreement.

What is BlazeStay's cancellation policy?

If you've booked a room but need to cancel before moving in, you can request a cancellation through your account. If the cancellation is made at least 7 days before the move-in date, you'll receive a full refund of any booking amount paid (excluding processing fees). For cancellations within 7 days of the move-in date, the refund policy depends on the owner's terms as mentioned on the listing.

How does BlazeStay handle disputes between tenants and owners?

In case of any dispute between a tenant and an owner, both parties can raise a complaint through our support system. Our customer service team will mediate the discussion and help resolve the issue fairly. We encourage both parties to maintain clear communication and document all agreements in writing.
